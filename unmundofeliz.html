GRAPHIC DESIGN | Un mundo feliz

Un mundo feliz

The redesign of the cover for 'Un Mundo Feliz'(Brave New World) delves into a thoughtful reimagination of Aldous Huxley's iconic work. Drawing inspiration from the profound themes of the book, the new cover encapsulates a visual narrative that marries dystopian undertones with modern design sensibilities. The concept navigates the delicate balance between utopia and dystopia, employing a color palette that reflects the juxtaposition of societal ideals. A play with symbolism and imagery captures the essence of Huxley's vision, ensuring the cover resonates with the profound nature of the narrative. Typography is thoughtfully curated to complement the thematic depth of 'Un Mundo Feliz,' offering a harmonious blend of classic and contemporary elements. This redesigned cover aims to entice both new readers and long-time fans, inviting them into the complex and thought-provoking world envisioned by Aldous Huxley.
