Graphic Design | Racer

Racer

"Racer" is a new brand of isotonic beverages. It's a drink specially crafted to provide rehydration to the body through its unique components. Targeted primarily at athletes who prioritize taking care of their bodies, Racer aims to fulfill the hydration needs of individuals engaged in physical activities.

VALUES
  • Hydration: Central to the brand, emphasizing the importance of maintaining proper hydration for overall well-being.
  • Healthy: Focused on promoting a healthy lifestyle, indicating that the product contributes positively to one's well-being.
  • Sport/Activity: Aligned with the active lifestyle, emphasizing the suitability of the product for individuals engaged in sports and physical activities.
  • Cheerful/Dopamine: Reflects a vibrant and cheerful brand personality, suggesting a positive impact on mood and well-being, potentially linked to the release of dopamine during physical activity.
  • Fluid/Dynamic: Expresses the dynamic and fluid nature of the brand, highlighting adaptability and a forward-moving approach in both design and product philosophy.
The design proposal for the Racer logo revolves around four key concepts:
  • R: Represents the Racer brand.
  • Drop: Symbolizes hydration.
  • Path: Represents the athlete's journey and the point of dehydration.
  • Goal (squares): Indicates the athlete's objective.

In the graphic symbol, these ideas are visually presented as follows: it starts with the drop, separated from the second element (the goal). The gap between them symbolizes the moment of dehydration, emphasizing the necessity of the drop to reach the goal. Ultimately, this combination forms the letter "R," representing Racer. Important factors include composition and color. The composition aims to be fluid and dynamic, with circles playing a significant role and adhering to the golden ratio. Regarding color, it should be eye-catching, contrasting with neutral tones to emphasize the ideas of movement and health. In this case, lime, white, and gray tones have been employed to achieve this effect.
