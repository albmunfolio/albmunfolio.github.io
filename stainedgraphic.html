Stained

This is a reel for social media created to promote "Stained," an eyewear brand focused on producing quality and sustainable products without losing its core objective: allowing people to express themselves. The name is derived from "stained glass," reflecting its colorful and bold nature. Additionally, "stained" means "mancha" in Spanish, emphasizing the brand's need to be vibrant and daring. It's ideal for those looking to stand out and differentiate themselves—suited for adults, young individuals, and those not so young, seeking color and fun.

"Stained" revolves around the following concepts:
  • sustainable
  • stain
  • irregular
  • expressie
  • fitting in
