Detective Evidencia

Showcase a gripping narrative centered around a peculiar incident involving a series of pigeon deaths. The unsuspecting culprit behind these mysterious deaths is an elderly woman named Pepi Croqueti, renowned for crafting the world's best croquettes. However, the twist in the tale unfolds as her former pet pigeon becomes a traitor, leading to her exposure. Inspired by detective series and the aesthetics of newspapers, the motion graphics aim to evoke an analog and painterly feel. The visual style seamlessly weaves between the realms of mystery and the bizarre, capturing the essence of the narrative. The storyline, with its unexpected turns, adds a layer of intrigue, making the credit titles a visually engaging and thematic experience that blends elements of suspense, detective work, and the unusual.
